Canada Surveys Dashboard

COVID-19, Labour Force, and Perspectives

This Rich Data Services powered dashboard combines data from Canada's COVID-19 Case Statistics , Labour Force Surveys and the Perspective Survey Series I on Impacts of COVID-19 public use files. It has been designed to demonstrate RDS benefits and flexibility in terms of combining data from multiple sources for visualization or story telling puposes and rapid design.
